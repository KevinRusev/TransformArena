TRANSFORM ARENA
===============
A survival game based on the theme "Transform".

How to play:
  You're trapped in an arena with waves of enemies closing in.
  Transform between three forms to survive as long as possible.
  Each form has different speed, toughness, and attack power.

Controls:
  WASD / Arrow keys  -  Move
  1                  -  Transform to Circle (fast, agile)
  2                  -  Transform to Triangle (ranged, mouse aim)
  3                  -  Transform to Square (slow, tanky, area damage)
  Mouse              -  Aim (Triangle form)
  Space              -  Attack
  R                  -  Restart (after game over)
  ESC                -  Quit

Tips:
  - Circle form is great for dodging large groups
  - Triangle form deals big damage at range
  - Square form takes less damage and smashes nearby enemies
  - Switch forms often to handle different situations
  - Enemies get tougher each wave so keep moving!
